Credits

This website was created by Meredith Hitchcock, Jordan Shedlock, and Ellen Jane van Wyk as the final project for Information Visualization and Presentation, a course at the UC Berkeley School of Information.

The visualizations were created with Tableau and are based on the US Forest Service wildfire database, originally accessed through the National Park Service, and historical drought datasets accessed the National Climatic Data Center. The website was designed using a one-page HTML theme created by Start Bootstrap.
